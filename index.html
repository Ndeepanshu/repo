FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the procedure to collaborate as a facilitator with TMHM ?

The procedure is elementary, just go to the sign-in page and fill in your details. Upon fulfilling the requirements of TMHM, we will contact you shortly. 

What is the minimum qualification and experience that is needed to be a part of the organization as an educator ?

To qualify for collaboration you must hold 3 years of experience in the teaching field. Understanding child psychology and intacting the attention of the user is a must. When contacted, you should be able to produce the certificates if needed. 

Do I need to create the courses ?

Yes, all the facilitators are required to create courses for learners on the portal in the respective fields.  

How much time do I have to give to learners per day ?

In the beginning, it is very uncertain to give specific timings. Ideally it depends on the needs of the learners and their queries, if any.  

Do I have to take up live sessions regularly ?

Generally, it is as per the discipline you choose and the time limit you set for learners for the completion of the course. You may take it on a daily basis once, twice, or on a weekly basis, it all depends on the discipline you choose. 

How would I know that if there is any sign-up for any of my courses?

You will be informed through your dashboard for the discipline you opt for teaching and per dashboard you will be informed about the number of learners enrolled for the course. 

Can I take up any other training assignments while I work with TMHM ?

Yes, being a freelance trainer, you are free to take up any other assignments. However, you shall use different content for your course titles, description, etc

Can I create more than one course from any category and conduct them simultaneously ?

Yes, as per your capability and qualification you can create as many courses as you want to teach through your dashboard. 

Do I need to record video sessions and send them to you ?

In the near future after finishing the live-session period you have to create customized video lessons for the learners.

When do I get my payments and what is the procedure for the same ?

Payments are made completely on the performance basis and as per the terms and condition, you sign with us when you are selected.
